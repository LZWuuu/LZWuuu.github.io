Recent Research & Engineering 近期研究与工程

  1. 001

    LAG Framework Evolution

    Implementing single-stage teacher-force training pipeline. Redesigning the training loop to unify distillation and generation in one forward pass, reducing both wall-clock time and memory overhead.

  2. 002

    HPC Workarounds

    Redirecting DeepSpeed Triton autotune cache directories to non-NFS paths on the Ibex cluster. Diagnosing lock contention and I/O bottlenecks caused by shared filesystem caching in distributed training jobs.

  3. 003

    LLM Architecture Analysis

    Analyzing the impact of different lm_head structures within the same model family on hidden state transfer. Investigating how projection layer variations affect knowledge distillation fidelity.

  4. 001

    LAG 框架演进

    实现单阶段 teacher-force 训练流水线。重新设计训练循环，在一次前向传播中统一蒸馏与生成，降低训练时间与显存开销。

  5. 002

    HPC 环境适配

    将 DeepSpeed Triton 自动调优缓存目录重定向至 Ibex 集群上的非 NFS 路径。诊断分布式训练作业中共享文件系统缓存导致的锁竞争与 I/O 瓶颈。

  6. 003

    LLM 架构分析

    分析同一模型家族中不同 lm_head 结构对隐藏状态迁移的影响。研究投影层变体如何影响知识蒸馏的保真度。
